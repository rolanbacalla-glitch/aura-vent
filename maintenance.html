Diagnostic Indicators

Signs Your System Requires Immediate Attention

air

Reduced Airflow

Noticeably weaker extraction in wet rooms or poor supply in living areas indicates clogged filters or failing fan motors.

volume_up

Unusual Noise

Rattling, humming, or increased operational volume often points to unbalanced fans, worn bearings, or ductwork blockages.

water_drop

Condensation Build-up

Persistent window condensation or damp patches signify the system is no longer effectively managing indoor humidity levels.
