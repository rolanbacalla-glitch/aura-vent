Company Overview

Mechanical Precision.
Atmospheric Clarity.

Since 2014, Aura Vent Ltd has engineered superior residential ventilation systems. We do not just install ductwork; we calculate, calibrate, and construct enduring environments.

Established 2014

Built on rigorous standards and uncompromising quality.

Our foundation is built on the belief that indoor air quality is an engineering discipline. We transitioned from general HVAC to a highly specialised focus on Mechanical Ventilation with Heat Recovery (MVHR) and Mechanical Extract Ventilation (MEV) because we recognised a critical gap in the residential market: precision.

Every system we design and install operates as a tightly integrated network, measured and balanced to specific tolerances. We partner with architects, builders, and homeowners who demand long-term reliability over quick fixes.

MVHR Systems

Mechanical Ventilation with Heat Recovery. Delivering continuous fresh air while extracting stale air and recovering up to 95% of the heat. Engineered for airtight, modern homes.
